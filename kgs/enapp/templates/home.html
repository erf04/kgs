The Beginnings of KGS: Our Journey in Dates

In 2020, during an international business trip and reflecting on our family's deep roots in date farming, we decided to visit a few stores to check out their offerings. To our surprise, we found the packaging unattractive and the quality far below what we were used to having at home.

As the new generation of our family, we decided to revitalize an important part of our heritage and childhood memories. We began our research, faced numerous challenges, and worked to resolve them one by one. Finally, we developed a plan!
